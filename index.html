How I Work

I prioritize clarity over cleverness, structure over shortcuts, and systems that remain understandable months later. I treat frontend architecture as engineering, not decoration, and I assume every project will be maintained by someone else eventually. Possibly me. Possibly angry.

My work reflects production constraints: performance budgets, evolving requirements, authentication edge cases, and users who do unexpected things at inconvenient times.
